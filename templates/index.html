Travel Smart in Gujarat

Essential tips and insights to make your Gujarat journey memorable, safe, and enjoyable

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather. Avoid summer months (April-June) due to extreme heat.

Weather Preparation

Pack light cotton clothes for day, warm layers for winter evenings, especially in desert areas.

Photography Tips

Golden hour shots at salt flats are spectacular. Respect local customs when photographing people.

Local Transportation

Book private vehicles for remote areas. Local buses connect major cities affordably.

Budget Planning

Budget ₹2000-5000 per day. Carry cash for small vendors and remote locations.

Emergency Contacts

Save local police, hospital, and tourism helpline numbers. Keep emergency contacts handy.
